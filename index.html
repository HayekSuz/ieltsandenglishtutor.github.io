About Me

My Name's Suzanna Hayek and I have been a teacher for 10 years. I trained to become an English as a Foreign Language teacher at Oxford and Cherwell Valley College in Oxford, England. I teach people of all ages - children, teenagers and adults. I have taught at Berlitz, Oxford Acdemy of English and spent most of my teaching career working at the British Council.

I have 7 years experience teaching IELTS courses to groups as well as one to one. I have helped students improve their IELTS score, specifically in writing.

I also teach general English, focusing on Speaking, vocabulary and grammar. I have helped professionals be more confident when speaking with native English speakers at work and write more acurate and sophisticated formal emails. In addition to students who now feel they are able to communicate comfortably in English while travelling abroad.
